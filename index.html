    Frequently Asked Questions

    How does your service work?

    Use our Website(form) or WhatsApp to let us know which product you want us to buy or collect from any location in South Africa, Johannesburg. We'll go to your chosen location, get your product and send you pictures. You then let us know if you want us to handle delivery or you'll send someone to pick up in South Africa.

    How does your delivery work

    Once your items are purchased or collected, we work with reliable delivery company to transport the products Mozambique. You'll be contacted the next morining upon arrival, then go to collect safely using the WhatsApp receipt we provide.

    How much does your service cost?

    After placing an order (WhatsApp or Website form), we'll send you a quotation displaying how much the service will cost, as it depends on the volume of the item

    How do i know my money is safe?

    Payment method is MPESA, and your payments are handled securely and transparently. We confirm items availability before purchasing, we send photos of the product and let you decide if you will take it or not, we also provide receipts for every order, and keep you updated throughout the process. Your trust matters to us, and we only work with verified stores and reliable delivery companies
